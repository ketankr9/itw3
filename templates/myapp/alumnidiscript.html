IIT (BHU) Varanasi Alumni

With a rich heritage dating back to as far as 1919 (pioneers of engineering education in the country by being the first to start degree courses in Mining, Metallurgy, Ceramics and Pharmaceutics) that has been its preserve, it is no wonder that alumni of the institute occupy the echelons of the corporate ladder courtesy a dynamic, ever-evolving system where priority is placed on improving the self and the society and where the sky is set as the limit.

Notable Alumni

  • Krishan Kant, former Vice-President of India
  • Pulickel Ajayan, a pioneering scientist in the field of carbon nanotubes
  • Sandeep Pandey, a Ramon Magsaysay awardee who started the Asha for Education foundation
  • Nikesh Arora, Senior Vice President and Chief Business Officer at Google
  • Ram Charan, Global Indian of the year 2010 by Economic Times
  • Arun Anant, CEO of The Hindu
  • Satyendra Dubey, a former Project Director at the National Highways Authority of India who was murdered after exposing corruption in the Golden Quadrilateral Project
For a list of eminent alumni Visit: www.iitbhuglobal.org
